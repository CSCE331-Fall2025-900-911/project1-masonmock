Nonlinear Optimization for Nanoindentation Tests

This project, built in Python, focuses on developing a nonlinear optimization algorithm capable of predicting the material characteristics of nanomaterials based on experimental nanoindentation data.

I completed this project as part of my role as an undergraduate research assistant for Texas A&M University's J. Mike Walker '66 Department of Mechanical Engineering.

Key features include:

  • Outlier detection and data cleaning
  • Accommodations for various test methodologies
  • Visualization solutions and reporting tools
