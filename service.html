Community Service

Welcome to my community service page! Here, you'll find projects that showcase my commitment to giving back and making a positive impact.

TAMU BUILD Student Supervisor

Founded in 2013, TAMU BUILD is a student-led organization at Texas A&M University. BUILD volunteers help convert shipping containers into full-service medical clinics and distributes them to communities across the globe that lack access to quality healthcare facilities.

I served as a Student Supervisor, overseeing the efforts of fellow students and ensuring that the construction process ran smoothly.

The Big Event Volunteer

The Big Event is the largest one-day student-run service project in the nation. Each spring, thousands of Texas A&M students come together to say "Thank You" to the residents of Bryan and College Station by completing service projects such as yard work, window washing, and painting.

As a volunteer, I participated in this Aggie tradition of service, helping community members with home improvement projects they couldn't complete on their own. This experience reinforced the importance of gratitude and community connection.
